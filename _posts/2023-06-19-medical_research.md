The coursera has opened for free an excellent course **Understanding Research** ([link](https://www.coursera.org/learn/medical-research)[^1]. While most of the examples in the course are related to evidence‐based medicine, they are relevant for any statistical-based research.

I believe this course is **obligatory** for every thinking person and is quite important for post-graduate students. 

[^1] Since the course examples are from the field of medicine, the full name is *Understanding Medical Research: Your Facebook Friend is Wrong*. Don't bother about it.
