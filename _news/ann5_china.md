title: Visit of graduate students from China

We welcome Zikang Chen and Yiwei Huang that were awarded a research internship for a two-month visit as part of the collaboration between the Shanghai Advanced Research Institute, the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Shamoon College of Engineering (SCE), Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Electronics. This visit is led by [Dr. Tom Trigano](https://en.sce.ac.il/faculty/tom) as a part of our collaboration in the field of spectroscopy.
