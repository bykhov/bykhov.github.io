Zikang Chen and Yiwei Huang were awarded a research internship for a two-month visit as part of the collaboration between the Shanghai Advanced Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Shamoon College of Engineering (SCE), Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Electronics. This visit is leaded by [Dr. Tom Trigano](https://en.sce.ac.il/faculty/tom) as a part of our collaboration in the field on spectroscopy.
